Research

My primary research interest is the algorithmic and theoretical foundations of diffusion models on general Riemannian manifolds, with an emphasis on applications to Lie groups, via techniques from optimal transport and differential geometry. I am also interested in accelerating sampling for discrete diffusion models for structured data, such as sequences and natural language, with applications to protein modeling.
