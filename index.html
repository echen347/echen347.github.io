Research

My research spans several areas within mathematics and machine learning. I investigate the algorithmic and theoretical foundations of diffusion models on general Riemannian manifolds, with an emphasis on applications to Lie groups, by employing advanced techniques from optimal transport and differential geometry. I also develop and implement discrete diffusion models for structured data, such as sequences and natural language, with applications to protein modeling. This includes engineering novel predictor-corrector sampling strategies inspired by k-Gillespie methods to enhance convergence and generation quality.
